Frequently Asked Questions

How to order?

You can easily place an order by contacting us via WhatsApp, phone call, or by filling out the enquiry form in the contact section below. We will discuss your requirements and confirm the order personally.

How many days in advance should I order?

For standard cakes, we recommend at least 2-3 days' notice. For custom theme cakes or wedding cakes, please contact us 1-2 weeks in advance to ensure availability and proper detailing.

Do you make eggless cakes?

Yes, absolutely! We offer delicious 100% eggless options for almost all our cakes and desserts.

Can I customize my cake completely?

Yes, we specialize in custom cakes. You can choose the shape, size, flavor, theme, colors, and even provide a reference photo for us to recreate.

Do you provide delivery?

Yes, we provide safe delivery across Chennai. Delivery charges apply based on the distance. You can also opt for self-pickup from our location in Anna Nagar.

How should cakes be stored?

Cream cakes should be refrigerated and taken out 30 minutes before serving. Fondant cakes should be kept in a cool AC room, away from direct sunlight and humidity. We provide specific storage instructions with every order.
